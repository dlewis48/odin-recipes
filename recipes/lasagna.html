How to cook Lasagna.

Make Lasagna the easy way,in just 50 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Meat-We will use ground beef and italian sausage.
  • Onion and Garlic-One onion and two cloves of garlic will be cooked with the meat.
  • Sauce-One can crushed tomatoes,two cans of tomato sauce,and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar-Two tablespoons of sugar to help sweeten the sauce.
  • Seaoning-Parsley,basil,salt,italian seasoning,fennel seeds, black pepper and orageno
  • Noodles-Either store bought or homemade will work.
  • Cheese-Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg-Helps to bind the ricotta

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it rest

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat then top with the remaining cheese

How long to cook the lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
