How to cook lasagna.

Ingredients

  1. Meat-We will use ground beef and italian sausage.
  2. Onion and Garlic-One onion and two cloves of garlic will be cooked with the meat.
  3. Sauce-One can crushed tomatoes,two cans of tomato sauce,and two cans of tomato paste
  4. Sugar-Two tablespoons of sugar to help sweeten the sauce.
  5. Seaoning-Parsley,basil,salt,italian seasoning,fennel seeds, black pepper and orageno
  6. Noodles-Either store bought or homemade will work.
  7. Cheese-Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  8. Egg-Helps to bind the ricotta
