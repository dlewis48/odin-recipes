How to make Spaghetti

Simple Spaghetti recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ground Meat
  • Tomatoes and tomato paste
  • Onion, garlic,spices

Steps

  1. Meat sauce-Brown your meat in a bit of oil, and then stir in onions until they soften. Then add lots of flavor with garlic, tomato paste, oregano, and red pepper flakes. To finish the sauce, add a bit of water (or broth) and crushed tomatoes.
  2. Pasta-Cook in salted water for 8-12 minutes
  3. Combine-Once your spaghetti is ready, toss it with your delicious homemade meat sauce and serve with some parmesan cheese sprinkled on top
